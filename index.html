Frequently Asked Questions

How does ClickShield AI differ from regular antivirus software?
Traditional antivirus software only catches known threats from blacklists. Our AI analyzes website content, layout patterns, and social engineering tactics in real-time to detect new scam sites as they appear - even if they've never been reported before.
Will this slow down my browsing experience?
No. Our lightweight extension runs in the background and analyzes sites instantly. Most users don't notice any performance impact while browsing.

We maintain specialized threat intelligence covering local scam campaigns, cultural context, and regional threat patterns specific to ANZ. Our database includes Australian shopping sites, local bank phishing attempts, and region-specific romance scams.
Can I use this on multiple devices?
The Guardian plan covers one user across multiple Chrome browsers. The Family Shield plan includes up to 5 family accounts, perfect for protecting your entire household.
What happens if I encounter a false positive?
You can easily report false positives through the extension, and our team reviews them within 24 hours. Premium users get priority handling of reports and can whitelist trusted sites.
